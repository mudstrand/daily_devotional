*Today’s Reflection:*

Whatever pops up, whether “good” of “bad,” we need

to pause, praise, and pray. We need to attend to whatever it is
without projecting
or jumping to conclusions, knowing Jesus goes with us wherever we go. I
admit it sometimes feels risky to not immediately brace for the worst, *but*
the peace I’ve gained through this practice

is priceless. *Whatever you face today, **bring it to *

*Jesus, giving thanks for the Holy Spirit's power to*

*never let you give up*
