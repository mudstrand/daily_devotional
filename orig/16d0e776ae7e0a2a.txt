*Verse for Today:*

*The Lord helps the fallen and lifts up those bent*

*beneath their loads. All eyes look up to You for*

*help: You give them their food as they need it.*

(Ps. 145:14, 15)



*Thoughts to Identify With:*

Sometimes our burdens are more then we can bear,

and we wonder how we can go on. God is, able to lift

us up because (1) His greatness is beyond discovery

(145:3): (2) He does mighty acts for each generation

(145:4): (3) He is full of mighty splendor and majesty

(145:5): (4) He does awe inspiring deeds

(145:5, 6): (5) He is righteous (Ps. 145:7): (6) He is kind,

merciful, patient, and compassionate (145:8, 9):

(7) He rules over an everlasting Kingdom (145:13):

(8) He is the source of all our daily needs: (Ps. 145:15, 16):

(9) He is righteous and kind in all His dealings (145:17):

*God* *is ready to lift you up.*
