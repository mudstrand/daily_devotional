*Today’s Verse:*

*I will tell everyone of your justice and goodness,*

*and I will praise You all day long. *(Ps. 36:8)



*Today’s Reflection:*

In contrast to evil people and their wicked plots

that end in failure God is faithful, righteous, and

just. His love is as vast as the heavens; His faith-

fulness reaches beyond the clouds; His righteous-

ness is as solid as mighty mountains, and His

judgments are as full of wisdom as the oceans

are with water. We need not fear evil people be-

cause we know God loves us, judges evil, and

will care for us throughout eternity.

*Pastor Sather*

*Ask for the Holy Spirit’s help, so you can con-*

*tinue to honor and praise the Triune God.*
