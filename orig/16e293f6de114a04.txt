*Today’s Verse:*

*“come, follow Me,” Jesus said, “and I will make you*

*fishers of men.” *(Mk. 1:17)



*Food for Thought:*

The fish symbol ICHTHYS is an abbreviation confession

of faith—shorthand for the Greek *Isus Christos Theou*

*Hyios Soter**: * Jesus Christ, Son of God, Savior.

The fish symbol is fitting because Jesus drew His first

disciples from fishermen, from two sets of brothers:

Peter and Andrew and James and John. Of course we

know, fish were used in some of His miracles.

As called and commissioned apostles, Peter and the

others went into the world as fishers of people to let

down the net of the Gospel, and they were effective.

“The disciples went out and preached everywhere,

and the Lord worked with them and confirmed His

Word by the signs that accompanied it” (Mk 16:20).

The ICHTHYS symbol is a reminder that Jesus Christ

is our Savior calling us to be fishers of people and

*“to give an answer to everyone who asks you to*

*give the reason for the hope you have” *(I Pet. 3:15).
