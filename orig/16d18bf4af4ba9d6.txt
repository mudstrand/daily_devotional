*Subject for 9/10/19 TAKE TIME TO LEARN MORE ABOUT GOD*

(read Ps. 147:1-6)



*Verse for Today:*

*How great is our Lord! His power is absolute!*

*His comprehension is beyond understanding! *(Ps. 147:5)



* Thought for Today:*

Sometimes we feel as if we don’t understand ourselves—

what we want, how we feel, what’s wrong with us, *or*

what we should do about it. But God’s understanding has

no limit, and therefore He understands us fully. *If *you feel

troubled and don’t understand yourself, remember that

God understands you perfectly. *So take your mind off*

*yourself and focus on God.*

*Pastor Al*
