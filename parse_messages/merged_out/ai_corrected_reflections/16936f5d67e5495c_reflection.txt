Since the Lord is holy, all His attributes share in that perfection. Let me explain what this means. As a holy God, He is perfect in righteousness, justice, truthfulness, and faithfulness. His perfect righteousness assures us that we can trust His will because He'll always do what is right. His perfect justice means that God will be unquestionably fair. His perfect truthfulness assures us that we can take Him at His word. His perfect faithfulness tells us that He will never let us down. We can have absolute confidence in our righteous, just, truthful, and faithful God because He is holy.
