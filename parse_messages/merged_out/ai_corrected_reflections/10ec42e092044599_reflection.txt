John, the most fleet of foot, reached the tomb first, and so he bent over and looked in, but he did not, or could not, cross the threshold. Some people who hear about the Resurrection for the first time often need time to comprehend the amazing story. Often, due to our daily walking in the world, we become accustomed to seeing before believing. But sooner or later, we have to come to have faith, because we are going to have to believe in certain things even though they are yet unseen. So the question that stands before us today is, "Do you have faith?"
