Sin can deceive people by misleading them by the law. The law was holy, expressing God's nature and will for the people. In the Garden of Eden (Genesis 3), the serpent deceived Eve, changing her focus from the freedom she had to one restriction God had made. Ever since then, we have all been rebels. Sin looks good to us precisely because God has said it is wrong. Instead of paying attention to His warnings, we use them as the "to do" list.
