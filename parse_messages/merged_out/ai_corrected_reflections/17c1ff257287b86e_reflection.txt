A man is deceived if he is a hearer of the word and not a doer (James 1:22). A man is deceived if he says he has no sin (1 John 1:8). A man is deceived when he thinks himself to be something when he is nothing (Galatians 6:3). A man is deceived when he thinks himself to be wise with worldly wisdom (1 Corinthians 3:18). A man is deceived when he seems to be religious and does not bridle his tongue (James 1:26). A man is deceived when he thinks that he will not reap what he sows (Galatians 6:11). A man is deceived when he thinks that evil company will not corrupt good morals (1 Corinthians 15:33). A man is deceived when he thinks that the unrighteous will inherit the kingdom of God (heaven) (1 Corinthians 6:9).
