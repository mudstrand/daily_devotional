Do you struggle with trust? Do you struggle with believing that Jesus can work in and through you when you doubt yourself? Let us remember that Jesus promises if we trust in Him, He will fill us with perfect peace. We need to rip our eyes off our fear of the unknown. We need to fix our minds on His goodness, strength, and purpose. We need to fix our minds on His great love, hope, and joy. Are you in an anxious, vulnerable place right now? Entrust your fears to Jesus today, knowing that He promises to fill you with His perfect peace.
