As sinners separated from God, we see His law from below, as a ladder to be climbed to get to God. Perhaps you have repeatedly tried to climb it, only to fall to the ground every time you have advanced one or two rungs. Or perhaps the sheer height of the ladder seems so overwhelming that you have never even started up. In either case, what a relief you should feel to see Jesus offering with open arms to lift you above the ladder of the law, to take you directly to God!
