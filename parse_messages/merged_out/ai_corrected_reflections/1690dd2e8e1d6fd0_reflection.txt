Have you found yourself in an environment that is not friendly to godliness? If so, what can you do? Here are some suggestions: Concentrate on God's goodness and depend on it. Stay true to your convictions. (Daniel 1) Immerse yourself in the Bible. (Psalm 119:49-50) Return good for evil. (Matthew 5:44) Trust God to be your companion. (Hebrews 13:5) When it's just you and God, that's enough.
