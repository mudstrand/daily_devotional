I think that it goes without saying, if we stop and think, that the world is full of enticements. Each and every day we are confronted with subtle pressures. The only way to escape these destructive influences is to ask God to help crucify our interest in them, just as Paul did. Let me pose this question to you: How much do the interests of this world matter to you? For more on this concept, look up Galatians 2:20 and 5:24.
