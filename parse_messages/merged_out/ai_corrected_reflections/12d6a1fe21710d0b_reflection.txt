Vs. 14 has very good advice if we want eternal life: "Seek good, not evil." Of course, we also need to remember that we are not saved by good works, but good works should follow faith in what Christ has done for us. The second promise is obviously what each of us is looking for: the Lord's personal presence with us. We need to realize that this text was directed toward the remnant of Joseph. But if we do not examine closely past history, we will fall to the same mistakes.
