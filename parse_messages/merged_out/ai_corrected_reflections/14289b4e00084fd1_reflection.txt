If ever two powerful forces were needed to preserve us along life's way, they are integrity and honesty. The psalmist asks for these to protect him step by step. Honesty makes us learn God's requirements and strive to fulfill them. Integrity—being what we say we are—keeps us from claiming to be honest while living as if we do not know God. Honesty says, "This is the Shepherd's way." And integrity says, "I will walk consistently in it." Are you walking consistently in the Shepherd's way?
