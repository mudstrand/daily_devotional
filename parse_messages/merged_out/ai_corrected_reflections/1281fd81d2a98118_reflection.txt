We have different occupations, live in different locations. But one name covers us all. "Children of God," John calls us, members of God's family, heirs of His heaven. But let us never forget that's not what we were by nature. "We were by nature objects of wrath," (Ephesians 2:3 tells us). How in the world could such a transformation take place? John excitedly tells us, "It was by the love the Father lavished on us." God's amazing love did the impossible for me.
