The world is full of enticements. Every day we are confronted with subtle cultural pressures. And it certainly does not help if we are addicted to the TV. The only way to escape these destructive influences is to ask God to help crucify our interests in them, just as Paul did. Just how much do the interests of this world matter to you? For more on this concept, read Galatians 2:20 and 5:24 (time well spent).
