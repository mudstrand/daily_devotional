Heavenly Father, we praise You for the trustworthiness of Your Word. Your every promise is true, and Your every word is flawless. We give thanks that we can rely on the unfailing faithfulness of Your Scriptures, for You are a God who does not lie or change. Help us to cling to Your reliable and life-giving truth, that we may walk in the light of Your perfect ways. In Jesus' name, Amen.
