Heavenly Father, we come to you with grateful hearts, recognizing your constant presence and the power of prayer. Help us to remain steadfast and devoted in our petitions, even when the answers seem delayed. Strengthen our faith, that we may trust in your perfect timing and the wisdom of your will. May our persistence in prayer be a testament to our confidence in you, the One who will never leave or forsake us. We surrender our concerns to you, knowing that your peace surpasses all understanding. Amen.
