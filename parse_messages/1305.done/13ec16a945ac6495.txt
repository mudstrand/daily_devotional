Today's Verse:



Show me Your unfailing love in wonderful ways.

You save with Your strength those who seek refuge 

From their enemies. . . Ps. 17:7



Food For Thought:



Yes God will save us-BUT let us not conclude, that we have somehow

missed God's protection if we experience troubles. God's protection has

far greater purposes than helping us avoid pain; it is to make us better

servants for Him. 

God also protects us by guiding us through painful circumstances, not

only by helping us escape them. . .
